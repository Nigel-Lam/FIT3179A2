Why Australia should make use of its massive Uranium Deposits

Currently, Australia holds 28% of the worlds Uranium deposit but is only responsible for 9% of the Worlds total Export. All of Australia's harvested Uranium is exported.

There is a lot of Uranium deposit located around Australia. However, only 3 locations are currently being actively mined. There are also multiple decomissioned mines

Uranium has the highest energy density out of all available resources in Australia. Even though it has the least amount of deposit, its high energy density allows it to last the longest if we only rely on Uranium. Due to it's high energy density, less of it needs to be mined to harvest equivalent energy as Coal or Oil.

Uranium's biggest environmental impact is the Nuclear Waste and Water usage. Nuclear Waste can be dealth with easily by storing it safely compared to Air Pollution and CO2 emission, which are hard to clean up.
